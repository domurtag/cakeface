ABOUT

OUR PHILOSOPHY
  • We believe that everyone should be able to apply make-up with the skill and ease of a professional.
  • Cakeface gives you insider tips and information. Teaching you how you can apply your make up that suits you and your lifestyle.
  • Cakeface will teach you how to shop for make-up that suits YOU.
  • We teach skincare routines to maintain healthy skin no matter what age you are.
  • We will advise on what products you are currently using and whether or not they are the best for your skin type and colouring.
  • We will update you on what is new on the market, what to avoid and what is a new must!
  • Our priority is healthy skin with make up as an enhancer to highlight the natural beauty you already possess.

Our Process

Our parties are tailored to meet your needs. Each person is given a skin consultation and is advised on their skin and what products are best suited to them.

We will do demonstrations of make up application on the looks that you particularly would like to achieve. Every one ends up with a full make over, in a way that they will be able to replicate again and again. Different skincare routines are discussed and demonstrated. We will tailor the party to the individual needs and occasions. Check out the examples and testimonials of parties we have done so far.

Our Guarantee

No matter if you've been wearing make up for years or are just starting out, you will learn new skills and be given invaluable advice.

Many women have been applying make up the same way for years. Skin changes over time as do the products and treatments available, keeping updated keeps you looking your best. For those starting out, Cakeface artists will make sure you don't develop any bad habits that can cause damage to your skin. We only ever recommend products that we ourselves would use

Our Mantra

Everyone is beautiful! Everyone has a unique look that needs a unique type of care. We will teach how to feel confident with make up and how to enhance your own individual style.

Maintaining beautiful skin makes wearing make up fun and empowering!
