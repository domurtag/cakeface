TWEENIE PARTIES

Tweenie parties...for girls who have started to experiment and get interested in make up. Tweenie parties are very popular as getting the girls early before developing bad habits!

The girls get to have loads of fun learning by practicing on each other and our birthday girl or host will decide what way she would like to be made up! Again, each girl gets individual attention and advice.Â  The girls love learning what suits them best and how to develop their own style! For young girls make up will become part of their lives (whether parents like it or not).

Our Cake face make up artists will teach them how to apply make up with skill and confidence. Knowing appropriate amounts and colours to suit their skin type. This is an important life skill for all women in our society. Learning what make up for what occasion is also very important, for example, job interviews require quite a different look to a girls night out! Every party is a chance to have a bit of fun with colours and looks. We will teach the girls how to look after their skin with advice on a skin care routine that will hopefully become a lifelong habit. Our cute party bags have a variety of sample products that we would recommend as part of every girls make up musts!
